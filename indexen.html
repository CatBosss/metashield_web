More than $600 million of crypto assets are stolen by phishing websites every year, including a large number of BAYC, Moonbird, etc.

Download Now

How do phishing sites steal?

Phishing sites usually disguise themselves as well-known NFT sites. If you click on their inducing button like mint, they'll actually use the ‘approve’ or ‘send’ method to authorize your asset or transfer them away.

How do we protect your crypto assets?

MetaShield can quickly identify ‘approve’ or ‘send’ transactions, check the security status of authorized addresses through blacklist and whitelist, and inform you of the consequence of clicks. It warns or even blocks high-risk authorization.
